  \item Page84,4:已知统总体X,求二项分布B(N,P),$0<p<1$,N为正整数，求N和p的\emph{矩估计}。\\
    $\begin{array}{c}
      E(X)=Np,Var(X)=Np(1-p) \\
      \Rightarrow \left\{
                    \begin{array}{l}
                      \hat{NP}=\bar{x} \\
                      \hat{Np(1-p)}=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2
                    \end{array}
                  \right. \\
                  
      
      \Rightarrow \begin{array}{l}
                    \hat{p}=1-\frac{\sum_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}{\bar{x}*n} \\
                    \hat{N}=\frac{\bar{x}}{1-\frac{\sum_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}{\bar{x}*n}}
                  \end{array}
      
    \end{array}
    $
